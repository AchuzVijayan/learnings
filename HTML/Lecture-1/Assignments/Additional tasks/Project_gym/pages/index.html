Our History

Cardios Gym was founded in 2015 by Akash Vijayn, a passionate fitness enthusiast with a vision to create a community-focused gym that prioritizes health and well-being. Inspired by his own personal fitness journey and a desire to help others achieve their goals, Akash opened the doors of Cardios Gym in the heart of Chirakkara, Kerala. Over the years, Cardios Gym has grown into a thriving fitness center, offering a wide range of classes, personal training services, and state-of-the-art equipment. Our commitment to community, inclusivity, and excellence has helped us build a loyal membership base and establish ourselves as a leading fitness destination in Chirakkara.

Key Features, Facilities and Services at Cardios Gym

  • Facilities:

    • State-of-the-art equipment: A wide range of cardio machines, strength training equipment, and free weights to suit all fitness levels.
